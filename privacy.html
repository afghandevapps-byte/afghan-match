Privacy Policy & Child Safety Standards – Afghan Match

Last updated: December 2025
Adult-only service (18+).
Afghan Match is a marriage-focused matchmaking service for adults. We do not permit minors on the platform.

Afghan Match (“we,” “our,” or “us”) values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use the Afghan Match mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide

  • Name and age
  • Gender and marital status
  • City/region location (approximate only)
  • Profile photos
  • Profile information and preferences
  • In-app messages
  • Identity verification status (optional)

b. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage and performance data
  • Push notification tokens

We do not collect precise GPS location, contact lists, or background activity outside the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Account creation and management
  • Matching with compatible users
  • Secure in-app messaging
  • Safety features such as blocking and reporting
  • App performance and reliability improvements
  • Sending notifications you choose to receive

3. Photos & Privacy Controls

Afghan Match offers privacy-first controls. Photos can be hidden until a match occurs. Some example profiles may appear while the community grows; these are clearly marked and never message users.

4. Safety & Moderation

Users may block or report profiles at any time. Reports are reviewed to maintain community safety. Afghan Match provides tools and guidance, but users remain responsible for their own interactions.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Information is shared only when required by law or to protect users and platform integrity.

6. Data Retention

Data is retained while your account is active. Upon account deletion, your data is permanently removed, except where retention is required by law.

7. Account Deletion

You may delete your account at any time from within the app. Deletion removes your profile, photos, and messages and signs you out immediately.

8. Children’s Privacy (18+ Only)

Afghan Match is intended for adults aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we learn that an account belongs to a minor, we will take steps to restrict access and remove the account in accordance with applicable requirements.

9. Your Rights

  • Access your data
  • Update or correct information
  • Delete your account
  • Manage privacy and notification preferences

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here or within the app.

11. Contact Us

Email: support@afghanmatch.app

Child Safety Standards

Afghan Match is a Social/Dating category application intended for adults. We maintain child safety standards designed to prevent child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) and to respond quickly to potential concerns.

1) Zero Tolerance for CSAE

We prohibit any content or behavior that sexualizes, exploits, endangers, or targets minors. This includes (but is not limited to) grooming behavior, predatory conduct, sexual content involving minors, and any attempt to facilitate exploitation. Accounts involved in such behavior may be suspended or permanently removed.

2) CSAM Prohibition and Response

Afghan Match does not permit Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM). If CSAM is suspected or detected, we will remove the content where possible, restrict the account, and take appropriate action including reporting when required by applicable laws and regulations.

3) Reporting & User Feedback

Users can report profiles, messages, or behavior directly inside the app at any time (for example, from a profile menu or chat screen). Reports help us investigate and enforce safety rules, including child safety protections.

If you are reporting an urgent child safety concern and cannot access the in-app reporting flow, email us using the dedicated contact below.

4) Enforcement & Review Process

  • We review reports and may request additional context when needed.
  • We may restrict features (chat, discovery), hide profiles, suspend, or permanently ban accounts for violations.
  • We cooperate with lawful requests and act to protect users and platform integrity.

5) Child Safety Point of Contact

For child safety concerns (CSAE/CSAM), contact:
Email: child-safety@afghanmatch.app

For general support questions, use support@afghanmatch.app.

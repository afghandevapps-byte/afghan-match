Child Safety & Protection – Aarosi – Waada

Last updated: January 2026
Adult-only service (18+).
Aarosi – Waada is intended exclusively for adults. We do not permit minors on the platform.

Aarosi – Waada has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) and child sexual abuse material (CSAM). Any content or behavior that exploits, endangers, targets, or sexualizes minors is strictly prohibited.

Safety Measures

  • Mandatory age requirement of 18+ for all users
  • In-app reporting and blocking tools available at all times
  • Priority review of reports related to child safety
  • Account restriction, suspension, or permanent removal for violations

Reporting

Users can report profiles, messages, or behavior directly inside the app. Reports help us investigate and enforce our safety rules. Reporting does not notify the other user.

If you are reporting an urgent child safety concern and cannot access in-app reporting, contact us using the dedicated email below.

Compliance

We comply with applicable child safety laws and cooperate with relevant authorities when required. Where legally required, we may report CSAM/CSAE-related matters to appropriate entities or law enforcement.
